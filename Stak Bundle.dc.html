Privacy Policy

Your privacy matters to us. All apps offered on UsefulApps.io — whether part of a paid bundle or available for free — are built with a simple principle: your data belongs to you.

Paid apps. Our paid apps do not collect, store, or transmit any personal data. All app data stays locally on your device or, where you choose to enable it, in your personal iCloud account. Purchases are processed entirely by Apple through the App Store — we never see or store your payment information. Paid apps contain no advertising, no tracking, and no third-party analytics.

Free apps. Our free apps follow the same standard: no personal data is collected and no user profiles are created. Some free apps may use basic, anonymized crash reporting or usage statistics provided by Apple's App Store framework to help us improve stability — this data cannot be linked to you and can be disabled in your device settings at any time.

No third parties. We do not sell, rent, or share any data with third parties. Our apps do not embed third-party advertising SDKs or social media trackers.

Questions. If you have any questions about this privacy policy or how our apps handle data, contact us anytime at privacy@usefulapps.io.
